Sponsorship Packages


RUM-Air invites you to be part of our continuous success in representing our country at an international aircraft design competition. To stay on par with everchanging technology, the competition regulations are constantly modified, which tends to increase project budget requirements. With your support, we will be able to stay one step ahead of the competition in aerospace innovation by acquiring state-of-the-art tools, materials, and manufacturing processes. The funds will be used to finance team registration, prototype and final aircraft manufacture (materials & equipment), competition logistics and travel, and team uniforms.

RUM-Air appreciates any kind of support your company can offer.
